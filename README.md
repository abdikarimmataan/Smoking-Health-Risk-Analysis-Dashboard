# 🚬Smoking-Health-Risk-Analysis-Dashboard

An interactive healthcare analytics dashboard developed using PowerBI, designed to explore the relationship between smoking behavior, patient demographics, and associated health risks.
This project transforms raw healthcare and lifestyle data into meaningful visual insights to support analysis, interpretation, and decision-making.

---

## 📁 Project Overview

The Smoking Health Risk Analysis Dashboard provides a comprehensive analytical view of:

  * Patient summary statistics
  * Smoking status segmentation
  * Smoking behavior patterns
  * Gender-based comparisons
  * Cholesterol & Hypertension risk analysis
  * Organ health storytelling (Healthy vs Damaged)

The dashboard is designed to support health risk evaluation, behavioral analysis, and medical insight discovery.
 


## 📊 Objectives

* Analyze patient demographics and health indicators
* Measure smoking prevalence and segmentation
* Evaluate smoking intensity across age groups
* Compare smoking behavior by gender
* Assess cholesterol & hypertension risk levels
* Enable dynamic comparison of Healthy vs Damaged states
* Deliver an interactive and visually engaging BI solution

## 📊 Key Visuals

  * 🍩 Donut Chart — % of Smoking Status: Displays Never, Current, Former smokers
  * 📊 Bar Chart — Smoking Status by Gender: Compares smoking categories across Male & Female
  * 📈 Line Chart — Smoking Duration & Daily Intake: Tracks YOS (Years of Smoking) & CPD (Cigarettes Per Day) by Age Group
  * 📊 Stacked Column Chart — Cholesterol & Hypertension Risk: Shows High, Low, Normal risk distribution
  * 🫀 Dynamic Visual — Healthy ↔ Damaged Toggle: Simulates organ condition comparison
  
  **🎛 Slicers / Filters: Age Group | Smoking Status | Gender*

## 📌 Insights

* Smoking Distribution:** Current and Former smokers represent a significant portion of patients.
* Behavioral Patterns:** Smoking duration (YOS) and consumption (CPD) peak within middle-age groups.
* Gender Comparison:** Smoking prevalence varies across Male and Female populations.
* Risk Trends:** Cholesterol and Hypertension risks increase with age.
* Health Impact Storytelling:** Healthy vs Damaged toggle visually communicates potential lifestyle consequences.
* Demographic Relationship: **Age and BMI variations influence observed risk levels.
